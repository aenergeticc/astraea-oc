Lore

Born in the slums of Gotham, Astraea watched as the people around her succumbed to the harsh reality of her homr city. The city known for its high curruption and crime rates directly affecting her and the people around her. What should have been happy and comfortable memories of Astraea surrounded by friends and family, leaning the ways of life, were replaced by harsh realies no child should experience, she taught herself to survive in the toughest of places. As a kid, astraea would stay up late to stargaze on hot summer nights with clear skies, she doesn't remember feeling peace, no, in fact she was always ready for any attack, but she remembered feeling at home with the stars. She snuck into libraries, studied whatever she could, she knew what she wanted to be now. When the time came for her to apply to university, she got rejected by everyone she applied to. She slowly realised that she may NEVER escape her current life, that institutes were searching for exactly the wealth that made gotham so corrupt. She'd had enough. She fought back the only way she knew how, targetting the wealthy, dismantling power structures, sabotaging, she even had blood on her hands from a few assassinations. It wasn't enough, the guilt gnawed at her heart. Now she walks a different path, breaking systems but trying not to break people.
